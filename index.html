Biography

I am a PhD student working on statistical transcriptomics methods in Sandrine Dudoit’s lab. I am collaborating with the He Lab, and recently joined a team in Berkeley lead by John Ngai as part of the NIH Brain Inititative, to apply those statistical and computing methods to current biological questions. I am also working on a joint project between Whole Biome and Laurent Jacob, developing metagenomics-related methodologies.
